Sir William Matthew Flinders Petrie

He was a well-known British Egyptologist. The name commonly used is Flinders Petrie who lived from 3rd June 1853 till 28th. July 1942.

Karl Richard Lepsius

He was a German Egyptologist, linguist and librarian. He led the Prussian expedition to Egypt and Nubia from 1842 to 1845, which is therefore also called the Lepsius Expedition.

François Auguste Ferdinand Mariette

He was a French Egyptologist. He was the founder of monument preservation in Egypt and of the Egyptian Museum in Cairo.

Howard Carter


He was a British Egyptologist who discovered the only virtually intact pharaoh's tomb to date, that of Tutankhamun, in the Valley of the Kings in western Thebes in 1922.


watch Howard Carter & the Discovery of Tutankhamun

Jean-François Champollion

He was a French linguist. By deciphering the hieroglyphic inscription on the Rosetta Stone and explaining it in 1824, he laid the foundation for the scientific study of dynastic Egypt.

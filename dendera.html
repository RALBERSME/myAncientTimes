Coordinates: 26° 8′ 30,6″ N, 32° 40′ 12,4″ O

Dendera is a place in the Upper Egyptian governorate of Qina with an archaeologically significant Hathor temple. Dendera is located just over 55 km north of Luxor on the left bank of the Nile opposite the city of Kena on the edge of the desert.

It is an architectural masterpiece that was built in the classical Egyptian style by the Ptolemies and Romans around 60 AD.

The temple of the goddess Hathor there, also called the Hathor Temple Dendera, is the only temple complex that can be visited in its entirety by visitors.

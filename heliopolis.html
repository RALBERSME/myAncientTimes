Heliopolis was an ancient Egyptian city in Lower Egypt in the area of ​​present-day Cairo, which existed from the predynastic era. According to ancient Egyptian belief, the world was created there. In addition to the main gods Re and Atum, Hathor, Horus and the Mnevis bull were also worshipped.

The oldest known temples there date from almost 4,600 years ago, from the time of the first pyramids in Egypt.

It is the seat of worship of the sun god Re.
